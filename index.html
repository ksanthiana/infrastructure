Our Mission

The Smart Infrastructure & Tourism Development Platform of Burundi relates to our mission to assist in the development of sustainable infrastructure and tourism in Burundi with the help of technological solutions to job creation and economic growth.

Tourism and infrastructure in Africa are directly interrelated: tourism needs roads, transport, information technologies, accommodation, safety preparation, and the internet. Consequently, by introducing a digital system that will combine tourism information with infrastructure preparedness, is possible to help Burundi and other African nations reach sustainable development and inclusive growth.
